Panda

A group of pandas is called an embarrassment of pandas.

Giant pandas eat and eat and eat. Every day Pandas spend 12-14 hours eating, which is more than half of a day.

Female pandas only ovulate once each year. During that ovulation period, they are only fertile for two or three days. That is one of the reasons why the population has dwindled.
